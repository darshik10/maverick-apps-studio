About Maverick Notepad Calculator

An App which works as both a notepad and calculator & calculates numbers in your notes automatically ! Looking for a way for the numbers in your notes to be calculated automatically? And also the lsit and calculations to be shared with someone? Maverick Notepad Calculator is your answer ! Now just add all the numbers you want in your notes and our Notepad Calculator will calculate those numbers for you automatically !

You can use our Notepad calculator app for all your note taking needs, as well as the ones which involve numbers and calculations ! Simply enter a phrase, and the solution will appear right away. Tap equals is not necessary. It functions similarly to a notepad or word processor. Multiple questions and answers are displayed simultaneously.It is considerably simpler and easier than a spreadsheet.If you make a mistake, just correct the incorrect expression rather than start over
